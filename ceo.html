Mrs. Blessing Okpokwasili-Lebechukwu is a distinguished and widely respected executive designer, structural couturier, and entrepreneur. As the primary driving force behind EPREL Services Nigeria Ltd., she has established a premium brand celebrated for its high-end artistry, complex fabric manipulations, and luxury bespoke tailoring solutions across Nigeria.

With deep foundational expertise in couture architecture and custom wedding dress styling, Mrs. Blessing has built a reputation for handling high-profile ceremonial garments, luxury Aso-Ebi coordination, and precision native attire. Her vision ensures that every production item leaving our facility matches rigorous premium quality guidelines and structured layouts.

Beyond fashion excellence, her executive leadership guides the operations of EPREL Services, driving the business towards rapid fulfillment standards, community impact through mentorship training, and strategic corporate partnerships.
